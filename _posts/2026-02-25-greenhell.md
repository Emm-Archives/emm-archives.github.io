### **Soup and Infusion Guide**

| Type      | Use                                  | Ingredients          | Steps                                                                            | Notes                      |
| --------- | ------------------------------------ | -------------------- | -------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | -------------------------- |
| Bone Soup | Fever recovery. Hydration. Nutrients | 1 Bone. 1 Bowl Water | Build Small Campfire. Place Coconut Bowl. Fill with water. Add bone. Boil. Drink | Strong early fever support |
| Boiled Water | Safe hydration. Removes parasites | 1 Coconut Canteen Dirty Water | Place 2 Coconut Bowls. Pour water. Boil | Use second canteen to store clean water |
| Antivenom Infusion | Snake. Spider. Stingray | 1 Bowl Water. 1 Plantain Lily or Tobacco Leaves | Add herb to water. Boil. Drink | Faster recovery than bandage alone |
| Fever Infusion | Fever reduction | 1 Bowl Water. 1 Amara Flower | Add flower. Boil. Drink | Rest if fever continues |
| Antiparasitic Infusion | Removes parasites | 1 Bowl Water. 1 Cup Mushroom or Soursop | Add ingredient. Boil. Drink | Removes more parasites than eating raw |
| Meat Stew | Fast cooking. Higher nutrition | 1 Bowl Water. 1 Fresh Meat | Add meat to water. Boil. Eat | Masks sanity loss from insects. Cooks faster than direct fire |
